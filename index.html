FAQs

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about working with YammTech — from how we work to what results you can expect.

General
YammTech is a full-service digital agency combining custom software development, enterprise IT architecture, data analytics, and digital marketing under one roof. We serve both private businesses and government agencies, delivering end-to-end digital solutions that drive measurable growth.
YammTech is based in Toronto, Canada, serving clients across North America and internationally. We work with businesses, government agencies, and enterprises — reach out to discuss your specific needs.
Simply fill out the contact form on our Contact page or email us at info@yammtech.com. We'll schedule a free discovery call to understand your goals, scope, and timeline — then present a tailored proposal at no obligation.
Software Development
Timelines vary by complexity. A standard web application typically takes 8–16 weeks from kickoff to launch. A mobile app ranges from 12–24 weeks depending on platforms and features. We provide a detailed project timeline during the discovery phase so you always know what to expect.
We're technology-agnostic and select the best stack for each project. Common choices include React, Next.js, and Node.js for web; React Native and Flutter for mobile; AWS, Azure, and GCP for cloud; and PostgreSQL, MongoDB, or MySQL for databases. We document everything so your team can maintain it going forward.
Yes. All software projects include a 30-day post-launch warranty period at no extra cost. After that, we offer flexible monthly maintenance plans covering security patches, performance monitoring, bug fixes, and feature updates — so your product stays current and secure.
Digital Marketing
SEO is a long-term investment. Most clients begin seeing measurable organic traffic improvements within 3–6 months, with significant ranking gains at 6–12 months. We set realistic expectations upfront and provide monthly reporting so you can track progress at every stage.
We recommend a minimum ad spend of $1,500/month for Google Ads and $1,000/month for Meta Ads to gather statistically meaningful data and optimize effectively. Our management fees are separate and scale with your ad spend. We'll help you build a budget plan that maximizes ROI from day one.
Both. Software development projects are typically scoped as fixed-price or time-and-materials engagements. Digital marketing and managed IT services are offered as monthly retainers because consistent, ongoing effort is what produces compounding results. We're flexible — let's talk about what structure works for you.
Enterprise & Government
Yes — government work is a core part of our business. We have served 10+ state agencies including MVA, DNR, DBM, and the State Comptroller's Office. We understand procurement processes, compliance requirements (FISMA, NIST, FedRAMP), and the unique operational constraints of public-sector IT projects.
We design and implement systems compliant with FISMA, NIST 800-53, FedRAMP, HIPAA, SOC 2, and ADA/WCAG accessibility standards. Our security team conducts thorough compliance audits and can assist with ATO (Authority to Operate) documentation for federal engagements.
